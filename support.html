Welcome to the ListAll support page. We're here to help you get the most out of your list management experience.

Getting Started

ListAll is a simple and powerful list management app for iOS and Apple Watch that helps you organize your tasks, shopping lists, and more.

Features

  • Create Lists: Organize your items into separate lists
  • Add Items: Quickly add items to your lists with photos
  • iCloud Sync: Your lists sync across all your Apple devices
  • Apple Watch: Access your lists right from your wrist

Common Questions

How do I sync my lists across devices?

ListAll uses iCloud to automatically sync your lists. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all your devices and have iCloud enabled for ListAll.

Can I add photos to my list items?

Yes! You can attach photos to any list item to help you remember what you need.

How do I use ListAll on my Apple Watch?

Install the ListAll app on your iPhone, and the Apple Watch companion app will automatically be available on your watch. Your lists will sync automatically.
